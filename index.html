About Us

About us

Users can calculate their General Weighted Average (GWA) by entering their name and five grades into the GWA Calculator. The system provides a rapid and effective method of evaluating academic performance by automatically calculating and displaying the GWA after the grades are entered.

How to use

Enter your name and five subject grades in the designated fields to access the GWA Calculator. Once the necessary information has been entered, submit the form to have your General Weighted Average (GWA) determined automatically. You will be able to see your overall academic performance as soon as the result is displayed.
